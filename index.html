Our Mission

The mission of the Computer & Circuits Society (C&C Society) is to expose students to the use and application of technology and circuits in the disciplines of computing and electrical design. Our mission is to encourage participants for all levels to use technology and design skills to collaborate on projects that involves research, design, implementation and management. This club will simulate real world scenarios where teamwork, communication, project management and budget management skills are essential. These skills will be enhanced for every active member of C&C Society while performing every steps for each project with the aim of applying knowledge to real world applications.

About Us

The club's leadership structure, ordinarily set out in its bylaws, is largely a matter of custom, preference, and practicality. The critical leadership positions are the President, Vice President, Secretary, and Treasurer. The club's officers and various event and committee chairs usually comprise the Board of Directors, which establishes policy and provides overall direction for all club activities. The executive committee often performs a long-range planning role and acts for the board of directors or the entire club between meetings.
